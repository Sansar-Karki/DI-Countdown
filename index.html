About

We created an immersive gameshow experience that combines engineering with theatrical performance. Our project features a custom-built game apparatus where contestants reveal drawings using invisible ink, accompanied by dramatic reveal curtains and an exciting light show.

Theme: Ink to Impact — Just as invisible ink reveals its true form under UV light, our project demonstrates how small engineering elements can create big impact. The theme reinforces the idea that what seems hidden or subtle can become powerful when brought to light.

Build Box (The Gimmick)

Custom-built game apparatus where contestants slide drawings into an input slot. Inside, 11 ultraviolet LEDs reveal the invisible ink sketch. An ultrasonic sensor detects the paper and triggers a shaking motion. Once processed, an LED pattern displays on top, and the contestant cranks a wheel to reveal the finished output.

Engineering Details

  • Input Slot: Slides into the build box
  • 11 UV LEDs: Positioned to illuminate the paper (395nm wavelength)
  • Ultrasonic Sensor: Detects paper position and triggers motion
  • Shake Mechanism: Servo motor creates randomized motion for better ink activation
  • LED Display: Shows pattern when complete
  • Output Wheel: Crank to display final result
  • Hands-on Engagement: Audience interacts with the crank wheel

Engineering

Components Used

  • Raspberry Pi Pico microcontrollers
  • Ultrasonic sensors (HC-SR04)
  • 11 UV LEDs (395nm wavelength)
  • Servo motors (SG90)
  • Cardboard & bamboo frame
  • Fluorescent highlighter ink
  • Polyethylene sheet (retractable curtains)
  • Mirror tiles (Razzle Dazzle reflection)

Invisible Ink Formula

Ratio: 1:10 water to fluorescent ink

Process: Mixed 13 highlighters with purified water. Tested multiple ratios for optimal visibility under UV light while remaining nearly invisible under normal light.

Challenges Solved

  • UV LED positioning for even coverage
  • Sensor calibration for reliable detection
  • Shake motion for better ink activation
  • Curtain pulley system smooth operation
  • Light reflection optimization

Team Choice Elements

Invisible Ink Pens

Theme: Ink to Impact

Stay nearly invisible under normal light and "come to life" under UV light for dramatic reveal. Best mixture: 1:10 ratio of water to fluorescent ink. Used 13 highlighters through testing.

Engineering: Custom formula using fluorescent dye, tested with 13 different highlighters.

Gameshow Background

Theme: Milky Way galaxy

Black background with planet colors and white highlights creating a "out of this world" vibe. Makes UV ink, LEDs, and light effects stand out dramatically.

Engineering: High contrast black backdrop with strategically placed colored elements.

Reveal Curtains

Theme: Magical Pen Reveal

Classic red theater-style curtains with two-segment pulley system for smooth "ta-da" motion. Introduce magical pens in dramatic moment.

Engineering: Bamboo frame with reinforced cardboard base, pulley system.

Razzle Dazzle

Theme: Light Show Celebration

LED flashlight on servo-driven arm sweeps in repeating color-changing pattern. Triggered when winner is announced. Reflects off mosaic mirror tiles on stage.

Engineering: Servo-powered arm, color-changing sequence, mirror tile reflection.
