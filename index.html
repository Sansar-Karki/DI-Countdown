Team Choice Elements

Invisible Ink Pens

Theme: Ink to Impact

Stay nearly invisible under normal light and "come to life" under UV light for dramatic reveal. Made from recycled highlighters with fluorescent ink. Best mixture: 1:10 ratio of water to fluorescent ink. Used 13 highlighters through testing.

Gameshow Background

Theme: Milky Way galaxy

Black background with planet colors and white highlights creating a "out of this world" vibe. Reinforces theme "Ink to Impact" - showing how small things can become powerful. Makes UV ink, LEDs, and light effects stand out dramatically.

Buildbox (The Gimmick)

  • Contestants slide drawing into input slot
  • 11 ultraviolet LEDs reveal invisible ink sketch
  • Ultrasonic sensor detects paper and triggers shaking motion
  • LED pattern displays on top when processing complete
  • Contestant cranks wheel to display finished output
  • Hands-on experience keeps audience engaged

Reveal (Curtains)

  • Classic red theater-style curtains
  • Two-segment pulley system for smooth "ta-da" motion
  • Introduce magical pens in dramatic moment
  • Kicks off "Ink to Impact" magic
  • Bamboo frame with reinforced cardboard base

Razzle Dazzle (Light Show)

  • LED flashlight on servo-driven arm
  • Sweeps in repeating color-changing pattern
  • Triggered when winner is announced
  • Reflects off mosaic mirror tiles on stage
  • Final burst of energy celebrating the achievement
